The name Zaporozhia means "beyond the rapids". Before the Dniproges Dam was built, the rapids on the Dnipro river were a notorious impediment to navigation.

Zaporizhia is a city on the Dnieper River in southeastern Ukraine. It's home to Khortytsia Island, a former Cossack stronghold with the Khortytsia National Reserve and the open-air Museum of Zaporizhian Cossacks.
